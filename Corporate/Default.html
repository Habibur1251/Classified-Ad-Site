What is Apner Deal?

ApnerDeal.com is an online place where a business can offer their special deals and discounts in a few click and it gets published to the world immediately. Sellers can also post a conditional discount. That is, you can set a condition that you will offer a discount only if you get certain number of response. This is a great way of promoting your business and products as you don't lose anything if you don't get customers. Our visitors are coming to this site everyday and telling others to visit here because they not only find special deals from our registered sellers but also from other sellers who published their discounts and deals through newspaper or flyer or other traditional media.

What kinds of coupons are available ?

- Pintable Coupon: These coupons are used for obtaining discounts when shopping at stores. Simply print out the coupon, take it to the store and show them and you will get the discount / offer which is absolutely for you!

- Non Printable Coupon: You don’t need to print out this coupon, simply visit the store and get the discount / offer as usual.

How do I use a discount coupon ?

When you find a coupon you want to use, just click on the provided link, either you will see a printable coupon or a non printable coupon. All coupons or discounts are time sensitive so make sure that you are availing the discount by presenting the coupon to the store within the expiry date.
